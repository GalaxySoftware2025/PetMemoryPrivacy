Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a pet profile?

+

To create a pet profile:

  1. Open the Pet Memory app
  2. Tap the + button on the main screen
  3. Enter your pet's name, species, and birthday
  4. Add a photo (optional)
  5. Tap Save to create the profile

You can create unlimited pet profiles with any subscription tier!

What are the storage limits for each plan?

+

Pet Memory offers three storage tiers:

  • Free: 20 GB of storage
  • Plus: 200 GB of storage
  • Premium: 1 TB (1000 GB) of storage

All tiers include unlimited pets, original quality photos and videos, and full app features. The only difference is the amount of storage space available.

How do I upgrade my subscription?

+

To upgrade your subscription:

  1. Go to Settings in the app
  2. Tap on "Subscription"
  3. Choose your desired plan (Plus or Premium)
  4. Confirm the purchase through Apple's payment system

Your upgrade takes effect immediately, and you'll have access to the increased storage right away.

Can I share photos with family and friends?

+

Yes! You can easily share individual photos or videos:

  1. Open the photo or video you want to share
  2. Tap the share button (three dots menu)
  3. Choose "Share" or "Save to Photos"
  4. Select how you want to share (Messages, Email, Social Media, etc.)

Are my photos and videos compressed?

+

No! Pet Memory stores all your photos and videos in their original quality. We never compress or reduce the quality of your memories. Your files are uploaded and stored exactly as they were captured, preserving every detail.

How do I delete photos or videos?

+

There are two ways to delete media:

Single item:

  1. Open the photo/video
  2. Tap the three dots menu
  3. Select "Delete"
  4. Confirm deletion

Multiple items:

  1. In the gallery view, tap "Select"
  2. Choose the items you want to delete
  3. Tap the trash icon
  4. Confirm deletion
