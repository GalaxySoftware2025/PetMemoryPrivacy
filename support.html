How do I create my first pet profile?
After signing in with your Apple ID, tap the "+" button on the main screen. Enter your pet's name, species, date of birth, and add a photo. You can also include details like breed, personality, and hobbies to make the profile complete!
How do I add photos and videos?
Open your pet's gallery, tap "Add Photos & Videos", and select up to 100 items from your photo library. Pet Memory preserves your original photo formats (HEIC, JPEG, PNG) and supports videos too. Live Photos are also supported!

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I manage my subscription?
Go to Settings > Subscription in the app, or manage directly through your iPhone Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel anytime.
Is my data secure?
Yes! We use industry-standard encryption for all data transfers and storage. Your photos are stored securely in the cloud with redundant backups. Only you have access to your pet's memories.
Can I share photos with family?
You can export photos and videos to your device's photo library and share them through any app. Direct sharing features are coming soon!
What happens if I cancel my subscription?
You'll keep access until the end of your billing period. Your data remains safe, but you won't be able to upload new content if you exceed the free tier limits. You can resubscribe anytime to regain full access.
Why aren't my Live Photos playing?
Live Photos require both the image and video components to be uploaded. Tap and hold on the photo to play it. If it's not working, try re-uploading the Live Photo.

üîß Troubleshooting

Photos won't upload
Check your internet connection and available storage. If you're at your storage limit, consider upgrading your plan or removing some old photos. Also ensure you've granted photo library permissions in Settings.
App crashes or freezes
Try force-closing the app and reopening it. If issues persist, check for app updates in the App Store, or try deleting and reinstalling the app (your data is safely stored in the cloud).
